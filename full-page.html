Before you write in

The questions everyone asks.

“I tried Notion before, and quit.”
Because you had to maintain it. Here the AI maintains it: you talk, it files, links and updates. The structure keeps itself clean, and a health check fixes any drift with one click.
“Doesn't ChatGPT already do this?”
A generic chat forgets between sessions and doesn't know your business. The Brain has structured memory in your own Notion and learns your voice, offers, customers and rules in a guided brand interview, on day one.
“I don't have time to implement anything.”
You don't build it from scratch, I do the heavy lifting. But yes, you need a few hours with me, on your real business: you bring the context (site, products, customers, how you work), I set it into the system, over a few guided sessions. Because it's your business, it can't happen without you.
“What if an update breaks something?”
Updates preview what they'll change, back up before anything structural, and never touch your rows. A sub-minute health check with one-click fixes ships with the system.
“How much does it cost?”
I don't work with fixed packages. It's a 1-on-1 engagement, a few sessions, on your business. Let's talk for 15-20 minutes: tell me where you are, and if it's a fit I'll tell you exactly how and how much. If it's not, you'll hear that just as straight.
“What happens to my data if I stop?”
Nothing moves. Everything lives in your own Notion workspace. Stop tomorrow and every record stays exactly where it is, yours.
