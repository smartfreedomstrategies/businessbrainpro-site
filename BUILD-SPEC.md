# The Founders' Brain — landing page build spec
Locked 2026-07-15. Source of truth for full-page.html and index.html copy.
Session: rebrand follow-up (thefoundersbrain.com), copy sharpening pass, no visual redesign.

## Buyer
The solo expert founder: coach, consultant, fractional expert, one-person agency.
The business is them; they are its memory. Small-team founders (2-10 people)
self-select in; the language never chases them.

## Awareness + traffic
Warm-to-lukewarm. Phase 1 GTM produces referrals (Anna, Razvan's network) and
own-content clicks only; no cold traffic planned. The page is a credibility
surface, not a cold-conversion machine: offer, steps, and fit-filter come early,
education compresses lower.

## The one promise
You stop being your business's memory. Every idea, client, sale and follow-up
gets remembered and acted on for you; the day starts with a 7:00 brief instead
of a mental inventory.

## The mechanism, named
**Owned Memory** — structured memory that lives in the client's own Notion, not
on our platform. Cancel tomorrow and it stays theirs. The 7:00 Morning Brief is
the daily visible artifact of that memory; Owned Memory is why it works.
Competitor test: "an AI that remembers your business" is claimable by anyone;
"and the memory sits in your own workspace" is not.

## Rule of One
- One idea: your business finally has a memory that isn't you.
- One feeling: exhale (relief; someone competent has got it).
- One action: send one email (Let's talk). No forms, no funnel.

## Offer
Done-with-you 1-on-1 engagement, a few sessions, on the client's real business.
No listed price; conversation-first ("Let's talk", 15-20 min fit call).

## Headline
Locked: **"Stop being your business's memory."** (objection-hook variant)

Variants held for A/B once traffic exists:
- B: "Your business finally has a memory that isn't you."
- D (former control): "Your business, with a brain of its own."

## Hero block
- Subtitle: remembers every person/sale/idea/meeting, acts on what it knows,
  Owned Memory (your Notion, not our platform, cancel tomorrow and it's yours),
  7:00 brief before first coffee.
- Checks: "Yours forever. It lives in your Notion" / set up 1-on-1 / useful by
  the first morning.
- Proof strip under the CTA: Razvan, 21 years marketing & sales, 11 in pharma,
  New York firm from days of manual work to hours per client. (Real, not DEMO.)

hero → problem → isnt → core (dark) → **start (new: 3 steps + CTA)** →

testimonials on the page (none exist yet; none invented). When first
done-with-you clients finish, testimonials slot in at section 7 (after "day").
